Welcome to Aletheia

Created by Delphi

Aletheia is a Chrome Browser extension tool kit with the aim of helping the user analyze news articles so they can determine the accuracy and credibility of news for themselves.

What Does Each Component Mean?

Title

This is the title of the article, as stated by the news site.

Author(s)

These are the authors of the article, as stated by the news site.

Summary

This is a summary of the article that has been auto generated.

Sentiment

The "sentiment" of an article is based off of the language that the author uses. When words have strong negative connotations, then they increase the negative setiment score. Likewise, the more positive the connotation of a word, the more positive the sentiment score.

Below is an example sentiment scale. The negative sentiment is marked red, the positive sentiment is marked green, and the neutral sentiment is gray.

Related Articles

These are articles with similar content to the current article. Their sentiment scales have been provided for you so that you can compare and contrast articles.
